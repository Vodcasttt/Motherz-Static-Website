Thekady

Thekkady(Idukki district) is a town near Periyar National Park, an important tourist attraction in the Kerala state of India. The name Thekkady is derived from the word "thekku" which means teak. Temperatures are lowest in the months of December-January and highest in the months of April-May.

Mullaperiyar Dam

Mullaperiyar Dam is built on the Periyar River and is one of the places to visit in Thekkady Kerala India. The dam is situated at 2,890 ft above sea level on the Cardamom Hills. The famous Periyar National Park in Kerala is sprawled around the reservoir of this dam.

Ramakkalmedu

Ramakkalmedu is one of the top Thekkady tourist spots that no one should miss on their Kerala trip. The place has a high significance among the locals as well as among the tourists.
